OFC Network Design & Laying

OFC — or Optical Fiber Communication — underpins the digital framework of the modern urban network. Winsome’s fiber-optic network design focuses on support of increasing bandwidth requirements in carrier and enterprise fiber-optic networks. From fiber optic cable to DWDM, ROADM, and packet optical transport platforms, fiber-optic network design technology continues to find a home in mobile backhaul, cloud services, data center, and other highspeed network applications. Fiber optic network design refers to the specialized processes leading to successful installation and operation. OFC network design is important part of the HTMS solution. It provide a communication link that connects all the field equipment that are part of HTMS with each other and Central Control Room. The networking topology for designing the HTMS is hybrid in nature wherein ring, star and peer to peer mechanism considering the project length and site situation. Winsome always maintains a redundancy throughout the backbone without resulting in network data broadcasting storm.
