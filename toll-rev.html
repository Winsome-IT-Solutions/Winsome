Toll Revenue and Assurance Programs

The ability of Winsome’s traffic management systems to maximize toll revenue for its clients indisputably makes it a market leader in this segment. It has developed a holistic approach to revenue assurance by assisting organizations in identifying revenue leakages and plugging gaps in the revenue cycle. Winsome’s toll revenue maximization efficiency ensures that there is minimal opportunity for leakage. Indeed, its Revenue Assurance Programme has been designed in a way that not only protects revenue loss but also to enhances operational efficiency. Its deliverables include:

  • Systems / IT Audit
  • Operations Audit
  • Fraud Detection & Preventive measures
  • Process Control
  • Equipment Performance Audit
  • Internal Operations Audit

The specialized services offered by the Toll Revenue & Assurance Programme include surprise checks by toll vigilance teams to keep track of toll plaza operations. It also helps in formulating toll plaza operations policy manual considering the organizational chart of the plaza. It has proved to be the perfect programme for organizing training sessions for toll plaza staff to motivate and improve operational efficiency; route patrols; and incident management.
